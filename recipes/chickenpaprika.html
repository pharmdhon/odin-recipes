Chicken Paprika

Description

On a cold winters day warm up with some of moms's famous chicken paprika. This dish is nutritious, hearty and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Marys organic chicken
  • Botson Fancy Rice
  • Paprika
  • Flower
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Avocado Oil
  • Cherry Tomato's

Steps

  1. Get the supplies
  2. Fry them up in a cast iron skillet
  3. Pour contents over fresh rice
